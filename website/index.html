About Socius

Socius is an open source Android application built by passionate developers who believe in privacy, beautiful design, and community-driven development. We use modern Android technologies and follow Google's Material Design guidelines to create an app that's both powerful and beautiful.

Whether you're managing hundreds of contacts or just a few close friends, Socius provides a smooth, responsive experience that respects your data and your device's design language.
