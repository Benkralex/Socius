About Socius

A modern looking, open-source Material 3 contacts app for Android. I wanted a great contacts app and didn't find a good one, so I made my own.

Socius is currently a local contact manager that works seamlessly with your Android device. In the future, it will support syncing with self-hosted servers like Nextcloud or a dedicated Socius Server to keep your contacts synchronized across devices.

Current Features

  • Read and display Android system contacts (read-only)
  • Create and manage local contacts
  • View contact details and information
  • Sync local contacts with Android system via SyncAdapter
  • Import and export contacts as Google CSV and Socius JSON
  • Track important events like birthdays and anniversaries
  • Beautiful Material 3 design with Material You support
